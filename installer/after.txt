To start using Email Notes:

  1. Start Outlook (classic desktop).
  2. Open any mail.
  3. On the Home ribbon, look for the "Email Notes" group and click "Notes".
     The notepad docks on the right side, next to your mail.

It works completely locally — no Microsoft login, no internet. Your notes and
screenshots are stored only on this computer under
%APPDATA%\EmailNotes.

If the "Notes" button does not appear:
  * Make sure Outlook was fully closed during installation, then reopen it.
  * A log file at %APPDATA%\EmailNotes\addin.log shows what happened.

To remove the add-in later, uninstall "Email Notes" from Windows
Settings > Apps, then restart Outlook.
