To start using Email Notes:

  1. Fully close and reopen classic Outlook (so it reloads its add-ins).
  2. Open any mail.
  3. On the ribbon, click "Notes" to open the pane.
  4. Click the pin icon in the pane header to keep it docked while you
     browse your inbox.

Your notes and screenshots are stored locally on this computer only. They
are never uploaded anywhere.

To remove the add-in later, uninstall "Email Notes" from Windows
Settings > Apps, then restart Outlook.
